What is LiiaDA?

LiiaDA is an Ethical AI Agent Framework intended for production settings where trust, transparency, and safety are non‑negotiable.

It emphasizes agentic orchestration with ethical guardrails, Model Context Protocol (MCP) support for tools and data sources, and RAG‑grounded answers when paired with our knowledge stack — without exposing proprietary internals.

Unlike stacks that optimize only for raw capability, LiiaDA foregrounds epistemic humility (knowing when to say “I don’t know”), ethical boundaries, cultural adaptation, and narrative coherence across longer workflows.

🧠

Epistemic humility

Systems that can honestly signal uncertainty instead of over‑confident guesses.

Ethical by design

Hippocratic‑inspired principles: do no harm, with pre‑gates and post‑checks on sensitive actions.

🌍

Cultural adaptation

Respects language and context so behavior fits the user’s environment.

💾

Built‑in memory

Multi‑layer memory for tasks, durable patterns, and preferences — described at a product level only.

RAG integration

Works with hidden‑brain for knowledge‑grounded responses (public RAG 4.0 positioning).

🛡️

Security by design

Aligned with the HALETHEIA security posture (e.g. Sentinel, babuino, aronly) for safer agent execution.

Ethical foundations

Directionally aligned with the Manifesto for Ethical Use of AI (MEIA) — a public philosophical framework developed over 11 months of research (May 2025 – April 2026).

🎯Transparency: decisions should be traceable and reviewable at the product boundary.
🤝Respect: user autonomy and consent stay central.
⚖️Fairness: contextual and cultural awareness by design.
🛡️Safety: “do no harm” as a default stance.
